About StratoSys Report

Our Mission

StratoSys Report was built by Semester 4 Software Engineering students at GMI as a final-year project. Our mission is to transform the way organizations generate and manage reports by automating the process from form submission to PDF generation.

Who We Are

We are a team of passionate software engineers dedicated to solving real-world problems through innovative technology solutions. Our focus is on creating intuitive, efficient, and powerful tools that make reporting tasks simpler and more effective.

Our Approach

At StratoSys, we believe in the power of automation and integration. Our platform seamlessly connects with Google Forms and Sheets, leveraging the best of both worlds to provide a comprehensive reporting solution that is both powerful and easy to use.
